Recieve a warm welcome to the Tic Tac Toe game.
This is an ancient game that has carried a lot of adults through their childhood.
Its the same old game that was previously played by hand using a pen and pad.

Rules

There are 2 players : X & O.
The first player to get their symbol in 3-tile sequential order wins the game i.e either vertically, horizontally or diagonally.
By default, the starting player is X.

Click on the button below and play Qic Qac Qoe the " Qoma " way, or so I like to call it.
